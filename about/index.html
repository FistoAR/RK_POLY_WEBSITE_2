WHO WE ARE

RK Poly Products we are a plastic injection moulding company incepted two decades back at Coimbatore to produce high quality plastic injection mouldings for both complete product and component parts. Since then, we are specialized in providing our customers with precision engineered products/components and have created repute for ourselves in the industry. Today, the company has progressed well in engineering precision plastic injection moulding sector & have grown phenomenally to cater to the needs & quality standards of international market. 7 years in food grain Industry & Evolved as the premier IML food grain containers manufacturer
